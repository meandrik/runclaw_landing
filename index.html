// faq

Questions.

Will it actually sound like me?
Yes. We train your agent on your existing content, your tone, your style. During onboarding, we dial in your voice until you can't tell the difference. You approve everything before it goes live.
How is my data kept safe?
Scoped permissions — your agent only accesses what you authorize. OAuth with minimal scopes. Full audit logs. You can revoke access at any time.
How fast can I be live?
48 hours after onboarding. We run in supervised mode for the first few days, then go fully autonomous once you're comfortable.
Which platforms are supported?
LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, Threads, and Facebook. Each gets content tailored to its format and audience.
Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. We handle everything — deployment, platform connections, training, monitoring. You just tell us about your brand.
