// faq

Common questions.

How is my data kept safe?
Your agents run with scoped permissions — they only access what you explicitly authorize. All connections use OAuth with minimal scopes. We don't store your content or credentials. Audit logs track every action your agents take, and you can revoke access at any time.
Do I need to be technical to use this?
No. We handle all the technical setup — deployment, platform connections, content training, and monitoring. You just tell us about your brand and audience, and we build your marketing agents around it.
How long until my agents are live?
Most agents are deployed within 48 hours after onboarding. We run them in supervised mode for the first few days to tune content and engagement, then switch to full autonomous operation.
Which platforms are supported?
Our agents work across LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, Threads, and Facebook. Each platform gets content tailored to its format and audience. We can also connect analytics, CRM, and other marketing tools.
