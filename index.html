// faq

Common questions.

Will it actually sound like me?
Yes. During setup, we analyze your existing content, your tone, your style. Your agent learns how you write and speak. And you approve every post before it goes live, so nothing publishes that doesn't sound like you.
How is my data kept safe?
Your OpenClaw agent runs with scoped permissions. It only accesses what you explicitly authorize. Full audit logs track every action your agent takes, and you can revoke access at any time.
How long until my agent is live?
48 hours after onboarding is complete. The first call is not onboarding. After the kickoff call, we begin the onboarding process: establishing connection to the computer and obtaining all required API keys. Once that's done, the 48-hour clock starts and your agent goes live.
Which platforms are supported?
LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Threads, Facebook, and Instagram. Each gets content tailored to its format and audience. The base setup includes one channel, and you can add more anytime.
Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. We handle the entire technical setup. You tell us about your business and brand, and we build your agent around it. The mission control panel is designed for non-technical users.
Can I add more jobs later?
Absolutely. The base setup is a foundation. You can add more social channels, new types of tasks, and grow your agent's capabilities over time. Think of it as onboarding a new employee who keeps learning.
